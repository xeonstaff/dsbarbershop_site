About Us

D's Barbershop was founded in 2019 as a traditional men's barbershop.

Drawing on a rich history of barbershop techniques, culture & tradition, we offer a complete lineup of hair & beard services.

Our reviews speak for themselves: hundreds of satisfied customers, coming back again and again, for the excellent service and world-class haircuts we're known for.

With two locations — at the Greene in Beavercreek, and the Flats near downtown Dayton— we serve a wide range of customers in Dayton.

Book an appointment today and enjoy the best barbershop experience in the city.
